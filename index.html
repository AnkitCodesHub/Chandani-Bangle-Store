Our Story

Where Beauty Meets
Tradition

Chandni Bangle Store was founded with a heartfelt dream — to bring the finest bangles, beauty products, and daily essentials to the women and families of Ekangarsarai. What began as a small corner shop on Jehanabad Road has blossomed into the most trusted beauty and fashion destination in the region.

We believe every woman deserves to look and feel her best. From hand-crafted glass bangles to premium cosmetics, aromatic perfumes to daily pooja items — we curate products that celebrate femininity, tradition, and modern style in one beautiful space.

Our mission is simple: deliver quality products at affordable prices, backed by warm, personalised service that makes every visit memorable.
